Aerodynamics

In 2018-2019 I was the aerodynamics lead of TJU Racing. For the team I reformed the general aero & cooling layout, improved CFD accuracy, settled scripts and templates for efficient design workflow, and explored various optimization techniques, most of which are still in use today.

Music

Inspired by the music of Olivier Messiaen, in 2019 I began learning to play the keyboard by myself. Since then I've been playing Hauptwerk digital organ on a minimal hardware setup with software aids (MIDI triggering, stochastic reverberation model, etc.). Before that I sang the bass part in Tongji University Choir.
